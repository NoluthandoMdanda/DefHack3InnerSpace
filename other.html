Depression: a common and serious mental ilness that negavitvely affects a person's way of thinking, feelings and habits. Furthermore it causes the feeling of sadness and/or loss of interest in activities that a person previously found joy in.


In the United States the leading cause of disability for people between the ages of 15 and 44 is Major Depressive Disorder.

Anxiety: a normal reaction to stress.
Anxiety disorders: a group od mental illnesses that cause persistent and overpowering anxiety and fear. The prolonged and excessive feeling of anxiety can heavily affect people's day-to-day activities and can be worsened with triggers.


Anxiety disorders are the most common mental illnesses in the United States, affecting approximately 18.1% of the population (age 18 and older).
